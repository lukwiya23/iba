HISTORY OF THE IAU

BACKGROUND

The Indian Association Uganda makes 100 years of existence this 2022. Therefore, The Indian Business Forum (IBF) is organising a Business Conference to celebrate and welcome a new era of investment and shared prosperity for a better Uganda

Indians have invested an estimated 1 Billion Dollars in Uganda’s economy during the past two decades.
According to Bank of Uganda and Uganda Revenue Authority, these businesses contribute about 70% of Uganda’s direct taxes.

WHY YOU SHOULD ATTEND THIS CONFERENCE!

Get practical steps to investing in Uganda

Meet directly with government of Uganda investment officers

Get first had stories from existing successful Indian investors in Uganda

Visit factories, industries, facilities of existing investors in Uganda

Tourism and cultural experiences and opportunities
